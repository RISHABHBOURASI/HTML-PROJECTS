Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Missile Man of India & The People's President"

About

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002–2007). He is widely respected as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. A teacher at heart, he inspired millions of students to dream and achieve.

Achievements & Timeline

  • 1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1969: Joined ISRO and contributed to India's satellite launch vehicles.
  • 1998: Played a key role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
  • 2002-2007: Served as the 11th President of India.
  • 2015: Passed away while delivering a lecture, inspiring till his last moment.

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
